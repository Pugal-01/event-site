Delicious Biryani Recipe

Aromatic, flavorful, and perfect for any occasion

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add chicken and cook until tender.
  5. Mix in tomatoes, yogurt, and spices.
  6. Cook rice separately until 70% done.
  7. Layer rice and chicken mixture.
  8. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 20 minutes.
  9. Garnish with coriander and mint leaves.

Tips

Use high-quality basmati rice for best results. Let the biryani rest for 10 minutes before serving for enhanced flavor.
